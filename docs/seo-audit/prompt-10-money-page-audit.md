__SECTION 2 — Four Categories of Issues__

__ISSUE 1 — CRITICAL: Widespread URL Duplication & Canonical Confusion__

The site has at least 8 confirmed or probable duplicate page pairs where non\-canonical URLs receive meaningful impressions — fragmenting ranking signals and forcing Google to guess which version to rank\. The commercial furniture installation page has both /commercial\-office\-furniture\-installation\-chicago\-il/ \(354 impressions, pos 28\) and /services/commercial\-office\-furniture\-installation\-chicago\-il/ \(983 impressions, pos 33\) indexed and receiving clicks — representing 1,337 combined impressions split across two competing URLs\. If consolidated, this page would be the second\-most\-seen page on the site after the homepage\.

— /commercial\-office\-furniture\-installation\-chicago\-il/ vs /services/commercial\-office\-furniture\-installation\-chicago\-il/ — canonical exists but link equity is split

— /the\-differences\-between\-high\-and\-low\-voltage\-electricity/ vs /blog/\.\.\. — 2,492 combined impressions for same post

— /artwork\-installation/ vs /services/artwork\-installation/

— /window\-treatment\-installations/ vs /services/window\-treatment\-installations/

— /company\-office\-relocation\-chicago\-il/ vs /services/company\-office\-relocation\-chicago\-il/

— /commercial\-office\-furniture\-storage\-chicago\-il/ vs /services/commercial\-office\-furniture\-storage\-chicago\-il/

— /about/ vs /about\-us\-chicago\-il/ — latter at position 6\.2 with zero clicks; wasted top\-10 ranking

— /contact/ vs /contact\-us/

— /modular\-furniture\-designs/ vs /blog/modular\-furniture\-designs/

— /space\-planning/ vs /services/space\-planning/

*Immediate next step: Audit all canonical tags in WordPress, confirm 301 redirects are in place on all old/duplicate URLs, and verify in GSC URL Inspection\.*
